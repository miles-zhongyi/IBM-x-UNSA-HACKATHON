# Pitch deck (Markdown stand‑in)

> Replace or export to PDF (`pitch_deck.pdf`) for submission if the hackathon requires a binary deck.

## Slide 1 — Title

**HealthChat AI (demo)**  
Grounded patient understanding over visit notes — IBM watsonx + RAG.

## Slide 2 — Problem

Patients leave visits with PDFs and portals they cannot parse. Search engines amplify anxiety and misinformation.

## Slide 3 — Solution

A **patient‑first** interface: timeline + labs + meds + **chat with citations** to the exact note excerpts.

## Slide 4 — Safety by design

Preflight rules block crisis language, emergencies, and **medication change** prompts from reaching the LLM.

## Slide 5 — Tech

- **Ingestion**: SQLite + Chroma, section‑aware chunking  
- **Chat**: intent routing, retrieval, watsonx generation (optional), translation  
- **UI**: Next.js 14, accessibility controls, Web Speech API

## Slide 6 — Demo

Three personas: post‑MI, diabetes longitudinal labs, hospice sensitive language.

## Slide 7 — Ask

We are looking for **clinical advisors**, **design partners**, and a path to a **pilot** with responsible AI guardrails.
